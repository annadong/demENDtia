Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for a study?

You may contact the researcher directly to sign up using the information listed under each study description.

Does this application give my consent for participating in studies?

No. Using this app is only connecting you to researchers. You will sign separate consent form for participation in studies, provided by the researchers.

How do I contact the researcher?

You may contact the researcher directly using the information listed under each study description.

How do I know my information is protected?

Your information will not be released to third-parties without your consent, such as your name, date of birth, and your location.

How does signing up for a study benefit me?

Your participation in studies is extremely valuable to scientists who are at the frontiers of dementia research. Scientific breakthroughs with your help will benefit people living with dementia, their caregivers, and people who are prone to this disease.
